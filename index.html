A letter & a codex · KJP

hi. it's me.

Right Here · Right Now

I've been shitty. I know I have. I've been distant, in my head, hard to reach — and I'm sorry. None of it was about not loving you.

I wanted to give you something better than another half-explanation. So here it is — everything. What I've been building, why I've been building it, and where it all comes from.

Read it slow. Click the doorways when you want to wander deeper. This is my whole world.

The Short Version

I'm building a company called The First Spark. It's a consciousness technology platform — software, writing, frameworks, and tools that help people understand themselves and the world they're inside of.

I built it alone. I taught myself to code through it. There are now over ninety tools live on the internet under my name. Real software. Real users. Real revenue.

It's not a hobby. It's not a phase. It's a company, and it has a real investor deck, a press feature, and paying customers.

Why I Built It

You all know what happened to mom. Most of you lived it longer than I did — I was two.

What you might not know is that in 2018 I started looking. And in 2020, during a quiet COVID week, I figured out what nobody had said out loud. I sat with that knowledge alone for a long time.

Then in the summer of 2025, something shifted. I'm calling it the raft moment. The grief, the silence, the years of half-knowing — it stopped being a wound and became a blueprint.

The whole story is in something I'm writing called Book Zero — my memoir. And the symbolic version of it lives in Whispers of Niagara Avenue, which I literally printed and walked down that street to distribute.

Everything I'm building now is the answer to a question I've been carrying since I was a baby: what do you do with what you survive?

"You build the thing you wish had existed for you." — the thesis, in one line

The Spark Itself

The center of all of it is something I call the Selector Model — my original philosophy. Four layers of how reality actually works: Physics, Metaphysical, Relational, Temporal. Think of it as an operating system for consciousness. Everything else I've built sits on top of it.

I know how that sounds. I know. But I want you to actually look at what's there before you decide. Click anything. Read a page. Spend three minutes inside it. The work speaks.

What Lives Inside

The world has rooms. Each one is a real, working thing on the internet — built by me, hosted under my domain, used by real people:

  • Soul Maps — personalized blueprints I generate for people. Numerology + astrology + the Selector Model, woven into a single document about who someone is. This is what pays the bills right now.
  • Sigilcraft — a digital forge for making sigils. Daily sigil, soul sigil generator, library, grimoire. All browser-based. All mine.
  • Rituals — the practice layer. Where the philosophy becomes a daily thing.
  • The Sparkverse — the community. Real humans who get what I'm doing.
  • Press Start — my accountability dashboard. Where I track the build in public.

That's not all of it. There are ninety-plus tools. The ones above are just the front doors.

"Every page on this list is something I made with my own hands.
Click one. See what I mean." — the proof is in the work

Where I Am Right Now

I'm raising a small round from friends and family — $300K for 20% equity at a $1.5M post-money valuation, on a SAFE note. The full pitch is in my investor deck.

I was also featured in Voyage Ohio Hidden Gems in March — that's not nothing. That's real press, validating that what I'm doing is worth paying attention to.

I'm not putting this page in front of you to ask for money. I'm putting it in front of you so you see. The quiet years weren't empty. They were the foundation.

What I'm Asking

Honestly? Just to be known again. To not be the cousin / sister / daughter / niece who went quiet — but the one who was building something while she was quiet.

If you click around the codex below and feel something stir — that's the spark. That's what I'm trying to give the world. I just wanted family to feel it first.

And if you want to talk about any of it — the work, mom, the years I went silent, the future — I'm here. I'm ready in a way I wasn't before.
