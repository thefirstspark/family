A letter & a thesis · KJP

hi. it's me.

Right Here · Right Now

I've been shitty. I know I have. I've been distant, in my head, hard to reach — and I'm sorry. None of it was about not loving you.

I wanted to give you something better than another half-explanation. So here it is — everything. What I've been building, why I've been building it, and where it all comes from.

This page is just for family. Read it slow.

The Short Version

I'm building a company called The First Spark. It's a consciousness technology platform — software, writing, frameworks, and tools that help people understand themselves and the world they're inside of.

I built it alone. I taught myself to code through it. There are now over ninety tools live on the internet under my name.

It's real. It's mine. And it's only the beginning.

Why I Built It

You all know what happened to mom. Most of you lived it longer than I did — I was two.

What you might not know is that in 2018 I started looking. And in 2020, during a quiet COVID week, I figured out what nobody had said out loud. I sat with that knowledge alone for a long time.

Then in the summer of 2025, something shifted. I'm calling it the raft moment. The grief, the silence, the years of half-knowing — it stopped being a wound and became a blueprint.

Everything I'm building now is the answer to a question I've been carrying since I was a baby: what do you do with what you survive?

"You build the thing you wish had existed for you." — the thesis, in one line

What It Actually Is

The First Spark is a few things at once. The shortest way to describe it:

  • Soul Maps — personalized blueprints I generate for people, blending numerology, astrology, and a framework I built called the Selector Model. This is what pays the bills right now.
  • The Selector Model — my original philosophy. Four layers of how reality works. It's the operating system underneath everything else.
  • The Sparkverse — the community. People who get it.
  • Book Zero — the memoir. Mom, the truth, the years in between, and what came after. The story I've never told straight through.

Where I Am Right Now

I'm raising a small round from friends and family — $300K — to give this thing real legs. I have a press feature, a working product, paying customers, and an investor deck.

I'm not asking any of you for money on this page. I just want you to see it. To know that the quiet years weren't empty. They were full.

What I Need From You

Honestly? Just to be known again.

If you want to talk about any of this — the work, mom, the years I went quiet, anything — I'm here. I'm ready in a way I wasn't before.

And if you just want to say hi, that counts too.

I love you. That part never moved.

— KJP
